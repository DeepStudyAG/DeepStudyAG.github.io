$$ x\binom{K}{x} = \frac{xK!}{x!(K-x)!} = \frac{K!}{(x-1)!(K-x)!} = \frac{K(K-1)!}{(x-1)!((K-1) - (k-1))!} = K\binom{K-1}{x-1} $$
